Indigo watched the chaos of the magic held by their siblings’ creations and shuddered at the mess. Creating a plane of mundanity and quiet, this darkened mirror of the Material plane is the equivalent of the Shadowfell. Their choice to intentionally block magic from their creations proved to be for the best, as their distance from Light and the Penumbra meant that magical power would have been rare and fragile, easily shattered by a being with too much ambition. Despite Indigo’s efforts, their planet has still devolved into chaos as the mortals fought for unneeded power and land.
