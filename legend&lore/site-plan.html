Overview

Purpose

This is an organizational website for all the information I have for my D&D campaign- My current idea is a main page that depicts the 7 planets of the universe I have made, and each of them is a button that leads to a child page with all the information organized in sections that drop down to show the informative text.

The main page will also have information about the universe as a whole, along with the two major gods (each planet was created and is presided over by a smaller god, all of whom are children of the main two).

I'd even be interested to add some sort of animation to the planets- some sort of shift/expansion when they are hovered over, maybe? And the first two planets orbit around each other, so if it's possible to get something like that to work, that'd be super cool to learn+make happen.

Audience

Honestly, my audience is myself. I need a way to organize all my work, and just having piles of google docs isn't cutting it.
