Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to share the joy of white water rafting with people who might not think that white water rafting is for them. We want to make outdoor activities attractive to more than just the typical nature-loving sort.

Audience

The target audience of this site is people who have never tried white water rafting because they think they wouldn't enjoy it.
